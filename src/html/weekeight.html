IL Learning

Task one

I had a look at Dolce & Gabbana's "Digital experience" website, in which they wanted to create an immersive experience for their customers to promote a perfume. I initially though this would be a great example of the use of sound and audio on a website, especially to create emotions and ambiance, but I was left somewhat dissapointed by the lack of audio! You navigate your way through an italian town next to the sea, and I was hoping to hear the rush of waves and bird song, but, it was not until I reached the 'Piazzetta' that you are greeted by a man, who speaks to you. This was the only audio ques, even when clicking on interactives there was no satifying 'ping' or ambient audio. With the hard work that has went in to the visuals, it is noticably let down by the lack of audio and sound. This is a good example of why audio can be a very important aspect of a multimedia project. To evoke the sense of 'being' somewhere, or 'feeling' something.


A visually beautiful website, but substantially let down by the lack of audio.
